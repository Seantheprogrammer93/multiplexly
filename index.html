Compliance is a Business Essential

Multiplexly is committed to supporting small and mid-sized businesses with honest, transparent payment services. We prioritize compliance, clear pricing, and ethical practices so your business can thrive with confidence.

  • Strict adherence to card network rules for agents and merchants.
  • No misleading "0% processing" offers or fabricated savings claims.
  • Surcharge and cash-discount programs only where permitted by card brands and state law.
  • All KYC, OFAC, MATCH, BSA/AML, and underwriting performed by our trusted processing partners and their sponsor banks.
  • We never request, store, or transmit cardholder PAN, CVV, or PIN data.
  • All compensation (e.g., referral fees or residuals) is disclosed before you sign up.
